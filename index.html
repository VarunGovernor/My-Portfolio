My Voters Hub

Political campaign management platform for Telangana elections

Flutter + Supabase

Problem

Campaign teams across wards had no centralized tool — spreadsheets and WhatsApp groups led to duplicated efforts and zero data visibility.

Solution

Cross-platform app with Smart Folder voter filtering, Telugu-first UI, Twilio SMS invites, and offline-first Supabase sync.

Architecture

  • Supabase PostgreSQL with Row-Level Security
  • Offline-first sync for rural field agents
  • Smart Folder restructured data model
  • Twilio SMS voter outreach integration
  • CI/CD to Play Store & App Store

Impact

  • Shipped to production on both app stores
  • Real-time voter tracking for campaign teams
  • Eliminated manual spreadsheet management
  • Telugu-first accessibility design
