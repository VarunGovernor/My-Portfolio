Landing pages: 1–2 weeks. Mobile apps or dashboards: 4–8 weeks. Enterprise CRM or multi-tenant systems: 8–14 weeks. Timelines locked during the Discovery phase — no scope creep surprises.

Both. I run Ham Tech Innovations specifically for startup-friendly pricing and fast product launches. Most of my work is with early-stage founders who need a technical partner who ships.

Flat-rate packages. Web projects start at $1,999. Full-stack apps start at $4,999. Pricing is transparent, no hidden invoices. You'll get a fixed quote after the Discovery call.

Yes. 100%. You get full source code, documentation, and deployment access on day one. No lock-in, no licensing tricks.

Every project includes 30 days of post-launch support for bug fixes and small tweaks. Extended maintenance and feature work available on monthly retainer.
